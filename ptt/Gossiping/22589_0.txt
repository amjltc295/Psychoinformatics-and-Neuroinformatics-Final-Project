日本預計2019年開始服役 一共42架

ＦＢ連結：<a href="http://bit.ly/2rBjgY4" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">http://bit.ly/2rBjgY4</a>

ＦＢ內容：

NAGOYA, Japan, June 5, 2017 – The first Japanese-assembled F-35A was
unveiled during a ceremony at the Mitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI) F-35
Final Assembly and Check Out (FACO) facility in Nagoya, Japan today.
Approximately 200 people attended the ceremony including Japanese and United
States government and defense industry leaders. The ceremony highlighted the
strong partnership between Japan and United States.
(Photo by Thinh D. Nguyen, Lockheed Martin Aeronautics Company)
日本名古屋，2017年6月5日 - 日本首批日系組裝的F-35A
今天在日本名古屋的儀式上揭幕由三菱重工所最終裝配和檢驗的F-35。
約有二百人參加了儀式包含了兩國政府及國防工業高階主管。
儀式上強調了日本與美國的密切合作關係。
