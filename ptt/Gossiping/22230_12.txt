Republican Congressman Says God Will &#39;Take Care Of&#39; Climate Change
共和黨眾議員說上帝會來處理氣候變遷的問題

A Republican congressman told his constituents that he believes God will
&#34;take care of&#34; climate change if it proves to be a &#34;real problem.&#34;
Michigan Rep. Tim Walberg said during a town hall in Coldwater, Mich., on
Friday that while he believes climate change is real, it is not something for
humans to solve.

在上周五一場演講中
共和黨眾議員Tim Walberg告訴他的選民們如果氣候變遷是真實存在的問題
那麼上帝會來處理，不是人類該來擔心的

&#34;I believe there&#39;s climate change. I believe there&#39;s been climate change
since the beginning of time,&#34; Walberg said. &#34;Do I think man has some impact?
Yeah, of course. Can man change the entire universe? No.&#34;
He continued: &#34;Why do I believe that? Well, as a Christian, I believe that
there is a creator in God who is much bigger than us. And I&#39;m confident that,
if there&#39;s a real problem, he can take care of it.&#34;

眾議員說:
我相信氣候變遷是存在的 從時間存在時 氣候變遷就存在了
但人類有這樣的影響力? 人類有影響整個宇宙的能力? 我不這樣認為
做為一個基督徒，我相信有超越人類的造物者存在
而我深信著 如果氣候變遷是實際存在的問題 那麼造物者會負責出來處理

As constituents grumbled, Walberg said: &#34;I don&#39;t expect you to agree with me
on that.&#34;
Most scientists in recent years have said that global warming is driven by
human influence.
It was reported on Wednesday that President Donald Trump plans to pull the
United States out of the Paris Agreement, the landmark 2015 deal in which
nearly 200 countries promised to address climate change.Under the deal,
President Obama committed the U.S. to a 26-28% reduction in annual greenhouse
gas emissions by 2025.

底下選民一陣騷動 眾議員說 我不期待你們跟我有相同看法
近年內大部分科學家認為氣候變遷被人類活動驅動著
但上周三川普退出了巴黎氣候協議
