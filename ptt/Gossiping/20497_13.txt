蔡英文總統之所以被爽民罵「蔡英九」一部份的因素是在於「國家名稱」上，以及「維持現狀」然後<span class="f2 hl">對</span><span class="f2 hl">照</span><span class="f2 hl">組</span>就是<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span>，但是人的記憶是有選擇性的，只記得他們想記、他們想要的片段，然後故意遺忘他們不願意接受的事實但是瑞凡歷史就是歷史，在怎麼選擇遺忘，我們一樣找的到<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">5</span><span class="f2 hl">月</span><span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">日</span>，<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">水扁</span>在中華民國第十ꔊ蘅`統就職演說中所宣示的「<span class="f2 hl">四</span><span class="f2 hl">不</span><span class="f2 hl">一</span><span class="f2 hl">沒有</span>」：

“
只要<span class="f1 hl">中共</span>無意對<span class="f2 hl">台</span>動武，<span class="f2 hl">本人</span><span class="f2 hl">保證</span><span class="f2 hl">在</span><span class="f2 hl">任期</span><span class="f2 hl">之內</span>，<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">宣佈</span><span class="f2 hl">獨立</span>，<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">更改</span><span class="f2 hl">國號</span>，<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">推動</span><span class="f2 hl">兩國</span><span class="f2 hl">論</span><span class="f2 hl">入憲</span>，<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">推動</span><span class="f2 hl">改變</span><span class="f2 hl">現狀</span><span class="f2 hl">的</span><span class="f2 hl">統獨</span><span class="f2 hl">公投</span>，<span class="f2 hl">也</span><span class="f2 hl">沒</span><span class="f2 hl">有</span><span class="f2 hl">廢除</span><span class="f2 hl">國統</span><span class="f2 hl">綱領</span>
0;1;32m與<span class="f2 hl">國</span><span class="f2 hl">統</span><span class="f2 hl">會</span><span class="f2 hl">的</span><span class="f2 hl">問題</span>。
我幫各位整理一下：
<span class="f2 hl">四</span><span class="f2 hl">不</span><span class="f2 hl">一</span><span class="f2 hl">沒有</span>的<span class="f2 hl">基本</span><span class="f2 hl">前提</span>：
<span class="f1 hl">中共</span><span class="hl">無意</span><span class="hl">對</span><span class="f2 hl">台</span><span class="hl">動武</span>

「四不」是什麼
不宣布獨立
不更改國號
不推動兩國論入憲
不推動改變現狀的統獨公投
「一沒有」是指
沒有廢除國統綱領與國統會的問題

<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">6</span><span class="f2 hl">月</span><span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">7</span><span class="f2 hl">日</span>，<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span>接見外賓時<span class="f2 hl">表示</span>，他願意<span class="f2 hl">接受</span>「<span class="f2 hl">一個</span><span class="f2 hl">中國</span>，<span class="f2 hl">各自</span><span class="f2 hl">表述</span>」；第二天，陸委會主委蔡英文說，兩岸未對此達成共識。

<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">3</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">7</span><span class="f2 hl">月</span><span class="f2 hl">4</span><span class="f2 hl">日</span>，<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span>在《每日新聞》的<span class="f2 hl">專訪</span>中說，<span class="f2 hl">他</span><span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">改變</span>他就任總統時所提出的<span class="f2 hl">四</span><span class="f2 hl">不</span><span class="f2 hl">一</span><span class="f2 hl">沒有</span>，<span class="f2 hl">他</span><span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">用</span><span class="f2 hl">公投</span><span class="f2 hl">來</span><span class="f2 hl">決定</span><span class="f2 hl">統獨</span>，未來也不存在以公投來決定統獨的狀況。

<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">3</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">1</span><span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">月</span><span class="f2 hl">1</span><span class="f2 hl">5</span><span class="f2 hl">日</span>，<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span>在《金融時報》的<span class="f2 hl">專訪</span>中說，（PS:請注意這段)：<span class="f2 hl">如果</span><span class="f1 hl">中國</span><span class="f1 hl">人民</span><span class="f1 hl">解放</span><span class="f1 hl">軍</span><span class="f1 hl">對</span><span class="f2 hl">台灣</span>試射導彈，<span class="f2 hl">四</span><span class="f2 hl">不</span><span class="f2 hl">一</span><span class="f2 hl">沒有</span>的承諾<span class="f2 hl">將</span><span class="f2 hl">不再</span><span class="f2 hl">有效</span>。

<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">5</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">月</span><span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">4</span><span class="f2 hl">日</span>，<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span><span class="f3 hl">與</span><span class="f3 hl">親民</span><span class="f3 hl">黨</span><span class="f3 hl">主席</span><span class="f3 hl">宋</span><span class="f3 hl">楚</span><span class="f3 hl">瑜</span>於台北賓館召開「<span class="f2 hl">扁</span><span class="f3 hl">宋</span>會」，會後簽署〈十點聯合聲明〉，其中第一至三點等於再次宣示四不一沒有：

“
依照《中華民國憲法》所揭櫫的國家定位，即為兩岸目前在事實與法理上的現狀，此一中華民國主權現狀必須受到兩岸與國際社會的承認與尊重。
現階段兩岸關係的最高原則應為「遵守憲法」、「維持現狀」、「共創和平」。<span class="f2 hl">在</span><span class="f2 hl">兩岸</span><span class="f2 hl">和平</span><span class="f2 hl">的</span><span class="f2 hl">前提</span><span class="f2 hl">下</span>，<span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span><span class="f2 hl">承諾</span>，在其任期之內，（PS:注意這裡的四不一沒有)：<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">宣佈</span><span class="f2 hl">獨立</span>、<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">更</span><span class="f2 hl">改</span><span class="f2 hl">國號</span>、<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">推動</span><span class="f2 hl">兩國</span><span class="f2 hl">論</span><span class="f2 hl">入憲</span>、<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">推動</span><span class="f2 hl">改變</span><span class="f2 hl">現狀</span>
0;1;32m的<span class="f2 hl">統</span><span class="f2 hl">獨</span><span class="f2 hl">公投</span>，<span class="f2 hl">也</span><span class="f2 hl">沒</span><span class="f2 hl">有</span><span class="f2 hl">廢除</span><span class="f2 hl">國統</span><span class="f2 hl">綱領</span><span class="f2 hl">與</span><span class="f2 hl">國統</span><span class="f2 hl">會</span><span class="f2 hl">的</span><span class="f2 hl">問題</span>。<span class="f3 hl">宋</span><span class="f3 hl">主席</span><span class="f3 hl">對</span><span class="f3 hl">此</span><span class="f3 hl">表</span><span class="f3 hl">示</span><span class="f3 hl">同意</span><span class="f3 hl">與</span><span class="f3 hl">支持</span>。<span class="f3 hl">
</span>
為提升國家整體的競爭力與政府的管理效能，有必要凝聚朝野共識進行憲政改革。陳總統及宋主席共同承諾，<span class="hl">憲政</span><span class="hl">改革</span><span class="hl">的</span><span class="hl">推動</span><span class="hl">不</span><span class="hl">涉及</span><span class="hl">國家</span><span class="hl">主權</span>、<span class="hl">領土</span><span class="hl">及</span><span class="hl">台海</span><span class="hl">現狀</span><span class="hl">的</span><span class="hl">改變</span>，<span class="hl">並</span><span class="hl">依</span><span class="hl">憲法</span><span class="hl">所</span><span class="hl">規定</span><span class="hl">的</span><span class="hl">程序</span><span class="hl">進行</span><span class="hl">修改</span>。

<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">6</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span>「<span class="f2 hl">廢除</span><span class="f2 hl">國</span><span class="f2 hl">統</span><span class="f2 hl">綱領</span>」<span class="f2 hl">跟</span>「<span class="f2 hl">國家</span><span class="f2 hl">統一</span><span class="f2 hl">委員</span><span class="f2 hl">會</span>」<span class="f2 hl">後</span>，<span class="f2 hl">四</span><span class="f2 hl">不</span><span class="f2 hl">一</span><span class="f2 hl">沒有</span><span class="f2 hl">在</span><span class="f2 hl">同年</span><span class="f2 hl">進入</span><span class="f2 hl">歷史</span><span class="f2 hl">的</span><span class="f2 hl">洪流</span><span class="f2 hl">中</span>。

我們從這段歷史在回頭來看蔡英文總統對中國的政策：蔡英文的「新四不」軟中帶硬，其中「三不」：承諾不變、善意不變、更不會走回對抗的老路，前三是向中國善意的拋出維持現狀，（PS:請大家注意這裡）但最後「<span class="f2 hl">一不</span>」就是：「<span class="f2 hl">不會</span><span class="f2 hl">在</span><span class="f2 hl">壓力</span><span class="f2 hl">下</span><span class="f2 hl">屈服</span>」，則是<span class="f2 hl">告訴</span><span class="f1 hl">中國</span><span class="f2 hl">不要</span><span class="f2 hl">胡亂</span><span class="f2 hl">打壓</span>、<span class="f2 hl">逼人</span><span class="f2 hl">太</span><span class="f2 hl">甚</span>。

所以從爽民最愛比對的對照組中，蔡總統真的是他們口中的「蔡英九」嗎？如果從歷史的觀點，我覺得蔡總統不但<span class="f2 hl">承繼</span><span class="f2 hl">陳</span><span class="f2 hl">前</span><span class="f2 hl">總統</span>在<span class="f2 hl">2</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">0</span><span class="f2 hl">6</span><span class="f2 hl">年</span><span class="f2 hl">之前</span>「<span class="f2 hl">維持</span><span class="f2 hl">台</span><span class="f2 hl">海</span><span class="f2 hl">和平</span><span class="f2 hl">穩定</span>」的基調，而且更明確的<span class="hl">告訴</span><span class="f1 hl">中國</span>，<span class="f2 hl">維持</span><span class="f2 hl">現狀</span><span class="f2 hl">是</span><span class="f2 hl">建立</span><span class="f2 hl">在</span>「<span class="f2 hl">國</span><span class="f2 hl">與</span><span class="f2 hl">國</span><span class="f2 hl">的s
</span><span class="f2 hl">[m基礎</span><span class="f2 hl">上</span>」<span class="f2 hl">而</span><span class="f2 hl">不是</span><span class="f1 hl">9</span><span class="f1 hl">2</span><span class="f1 hl">共識</span>！

所以少數的爽民們，2000年時你可能還小，還是你長大了，但是一看到「蔡英文」就腦充血，選擇性的遺忘這段歷史，但是事實終究是事實，如果連史料都拿出來，然後你們還一昧的認為「地球是因為你們而公轉」然後繼續告訴別人蔡總統是蔡英九嗯～～～那我也只好繼續找資料來反駁你們。
